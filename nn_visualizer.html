Training

How this network learned

Every weight above was shaped by train.py: five full passes over the MNIST training set, taking one small downhill step with Adam after each batch of 64 images. Load trained weights pours those learned numbers into this page — no retraining happens, they were computed once and stored.

D · Output-layer weight gradient (fc2)

dL/dW for every fc2 weight — 10 digit rows × 128 hidden columns. Green = +, red = −. The true digit's row is mostly red: its weights get pushed up.
